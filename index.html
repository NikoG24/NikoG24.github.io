Lock Wizard

I developed a VR training module in Unity, guiding users through tasks within a lifelike virtual environment. I collaborated closely with a team of four to ensure an impactful VR experience, taking the lead on game element design. Utilizing a 360 camera, we enhanced our virtual reality environment in Unity, focusing on creating immersive experiences. Specifically, I designed interactive elements such as locks and lock-picking tools, including rakes and hooks.

Data Visualization Website

Designed and implemented interactive data dashboards using HTML and JavaScript to monitor performance metrics and analyze trends in NFL quarterback performance. The dashboards compared quarterbacks from both teams in each Super Bowl, allowing users to see how their seasons aligned and to compare them with quarterbacks from past Super Bowls. I translated complex statistical data into intuitive, user-friendly visualizations for easy comparison and analysis.

Superposition

For this project, we were given an abstract concept and tasked with transforming it into a storyboard book. My group's concept focused on superposition, which we illustrated through a creative superhero narrative. In our story, the superhero uses the principles of superposition to battle a powerful crime boss. As part of the project, we also developed a mini-game incorporating the concept of superposition. I contributed to both the narrative and the design, creating specific elements showcased in the art tab under storyboard section.

Goblin Teasure Run

This was my first experience developing a game. I started with a platformer game stub provided in class and built upon it with my own designs. I created the characters, background art, and several C# scripting elements. One of the key features I implemented was enemy behavior-specifically, coding enemies to detect and shoot at the player as they move through the environment. The shooting mechanic included player tracking to make the gameplay more challenging and engaging.In the art section of my portfolio I have some of the art I used in this project and also have a trailer I made to show case the game below.
